Mailing list

Get help using S2Graph or contribute to the project on our mailing lists:

  • users@s2graph.incubator.apache.org is for usage questions, help, and announcements. subscribe, unsubscribe, archives
  • dev@s2graph.incubator.apache.org is for people who want to contribute code to S2Graph. subscribe, unsubscribe, archives
  • commits@s2graph.incubator.apache.org is for commit messages and patches to S2Graph. subscribe, unsubscribe, archives
